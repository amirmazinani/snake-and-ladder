Prime Game

This game is a computational game, and all its parts are based on four basic mathematical acts.

How to play:

- You are blue. By knocking the roll button, two numbers are randomly displayed from 0 to 6 on the sides of the button.

- Now you must select one of the addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division operators to apply to the two numbers previously shown.

- By clicking on each operator, the number on the left is summed up or reduced, or multiplied, or divided by the right number, and our square of blue is equal to the result of the operation that goes back or forth, Or remains.

If any of the following happen, you go to start house:

1- Enter to the house with prime number.

2- Enter to the house with number less than 1

3- Enter to the house with number bigger than 100

4- The result of your operation is unspecified (This means that you divide the number to zero)

Tip: You can enter the house in which the opponent is in it, with this you will throw him to start home and you will be placed in opponent`s house.
